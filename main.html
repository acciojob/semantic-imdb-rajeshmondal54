Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story revolves around a thief, Dom Cobb, who has the ability to enter people's dreams and steal their secrets. He is given a chance to erase his criminal history by performing an almost impossible task: planting an idea into someone's subconscious.

Actors

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
  • Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer
